FAQ

How can I see the statistics page for a certain habit?

Just tap on the habit on the dashboard!

How can I set reminders?

Visit your profile and select the options wheel in the top right corner and then enable notifications how you want them.

How can I add more habits to the dashboard?

Click the plus icon on the bottom navigation bar or on the dashboard.

Will I lose my data if I delete the app?

No, all data is stored on our secure cloud and is tied to your account so it is accessible for you from anywhere!
